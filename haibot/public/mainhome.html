Hai Bot Recommender

  • Welcome to Haibot! A chatbot application for recommending hotels! (Only in Colombo for now!)

  • In order to talk to me, you need to say what type of hotel you prefer!

  • For example you could say "I want a hotel with a minibar, swimming pool, free WiFi and free parking"

  • Once you're done just type the words "recommend" (Make sure you asked for at least three suggestions)

  • Make sure you type with proper spelling and capitalization for names (Example: McDollars Haibot)

  • Now click on chat on the side bar so we can start chatting already!
